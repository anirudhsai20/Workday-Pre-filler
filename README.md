DESCRIPTION: 
This is a simple chrome extension that auto fills Workday applications based on the ionput enetred in the input form.

HOW TO SET IT UP?
1. Download the folder.
2. Open Chrome.
3. Click on the kabab menu (three dots) and click on 'Extensions'.
4. Click on 'Manage Extensions'.
5. Click on 'Load unpacked'.
6. Navigate to where the folder is downloaded and click on 'Select folder'.


HOW TO USE THE EXTENSION?
1. Open Chrome.
2. Click on the extensions icon.
3. Click on the kabab menu (three dots) of 'Workday Prefiller'.
4. Click on 'Options'.
5. Fill the required fields in the input form and click on 'Save'.
6. Close the form.
7. Open any Workday job application and click on apply.
8. Now, click on the extensions icon and click on 'Workday Prefiller'.
9. The fields will be filled automatically.
10. After a page is filled , click on 'Save and next' and click on the exntension the fill the page.
